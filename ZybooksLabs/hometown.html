Scottville, USA

Locations

Scottville is about 9 miles East of Ludington, right on coast of the Michigan Lake.

About

Weather

Being apart of the Michigan, it varies greatly throughout the year. Due to Lake in particular though, you see a lot of lake effect snow and rain.

Population

Scottville has 1,400 residents as of 2023.

Sports

MCC both sports basketball and Football. And McPhail Field offers baseball fields to the public.

Things To Do

  • Fish at the Riverside park
  • See famous Clown Band
  • Enjoy wonderful eateries
  • Float the Pere Marquette River
