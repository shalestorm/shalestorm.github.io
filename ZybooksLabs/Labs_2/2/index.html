Joji Fan Webpage

George Kusunoki Miller, or known popularly as Joji is a Japanese songwriter who started his serious music career in 2015.
He previously released an underground "secret" album while under his previous alias Filthy Frank.
However he would go on to scrap this album after two singles in favor of focusing on his Pink Guy mixtape.
It wasn't until 2017 when George would dawn the stage name Joji, using the popularity of his youtube
to excell his career faster. He was quickly signed in 2017 to 88rising, the label in which he started releasing his albums.
His first album under the label was In Tongues featuring his singles; Will he, Rain on Me, and I Don't Wanna Waste My Time
This was only the start! Since his debut Joji has managed to hit Top 10 Bilboard, ranking at 8 for 6 consecutive weeks. As well as boasting multiple Nominees and awards for album of the year.

You can find his official website for merch and tour dates. Along with his Wikipedia page to learn more.
